NotaQu Shelter

NotaQu Shelter is an automated operational expense recording application. Users submit receipts via Telegram, and NotaQu organizes receipt files and structured records using Google Drive, Google Sheets, and Google Docs. NotaQu may also send outbound emails with generated documents from an authorized administrative account.

What NotaQu Shelter Does

  • Receipt submission via Telegram for centralized collection
  • AI-powered extraction of key fields (date, amount, merchant, category)
  • Google Drive (drive.file) stores receipt files and generated documents
  • Google Sheets stores structured expense records for tracking and reconciliation
  • Google Docs generates print-ready documents with embedded receipt images and metadata
  • Gmail (send only) sends outbound emails with generated documents from an admin account
